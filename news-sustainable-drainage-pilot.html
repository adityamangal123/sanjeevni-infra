Sustainable drainage pilot expanded across urban packages

2025-12-02

Low-maintenance drainage features are now integrated in multiple city corridor projects.

Sanjeevni Infra has expanded its sustainable drainage pilot across multiple urban infrastructure packages. The initiative focuses on low-maintenance features that help manage runoff, improve corridor resilience, and reduce recurring maintenance pressure.

The drainage approach considers site levels, local rainfall patterns, pedestrian edges, road safety, and maintainability. By integrating these features earlier in planning, teams can avoid late-stage redesign and improve long-term asset performance.

The expansion reflects Sanjeevni Infra commitment to infrastructure that performs reliably beyond construction completion.

Key Focus Areas

  • Sustainable drainage features for urban corridors
  • Lower maintenance pressure through better planning
  • Improved long-term resilience of city infrastructure
