Sanjeevni Infra wins new highway package

2026-03-10

A 62 km package focused on resilient pavements and safety assets.

Sanjeevni Infra has secured a new highway package covering 62 km of corridor development. The package focuses on durable pavement systems, safer traffic movement, and practical construction sequencing for reliable delivery.

The work will include pavement strengthening, roadside safety assets, drainage coordination, and quality-led execution controls. The project team will apply structured inspection routines and progress tracking to maintain milestone visibility from mobilization through completion.

This award reinforces Sanjeevni Infra as a trusted infrastructure partner for road and highway development across India.

Key Focus Areas

  • 62 km highway development package
  • Resilient pavement and drainage-led execution
  • Safety assets planned as part of corridor delivery

Sanjeevni Infra Projects Limited continues to combine field execution discipline with transparent communication, ensuring every project milestone is backed by planning, quality checks, and accountable delivery.
