About Us

Since almost two decades, building India's infrastructure with integrity and trust.

Our Journey

Sanjeevni Infra Projects Limited (SIPL) has been at the forefront of infrastructure development in India since its incorporation in 2007. For almost two decades, we have been committed to creating roads, highways, and buildings that stand the test of time.

Our journey is one of relentless growth and unwavering commitment to quality. We work with some of the most prestigious government organizations in India, delivering projects that shape the nation's infrastructure landscape.

In January 2025, M/s Wonder Infrastructure & Power, a proprietary concern with a strong track record, was fully integrated into SIPL, further strengthening our capabilities and expanding our project portfolio.

In Loving Memory

Legacy of Our Founder

When Sanjeevni Infra Projects Limited was founded in 2007, it was not just the beginning of a business. It was the beginning of a vision shaped by integrity, resilience, and an unshakable belief in trust.

Late Mr. Sanjeev Kumar Burman was a man of deeply rooted values. He believed that anything built on honesty and commitment would stand the test of time. For him, success was never measured merely by growth, but by the respect the organisation earned and the trust it upheld.

In the formative years of the Company, he instilled a culture where commitments were sacred, relationships were nurtured with sincerity, and credibility was never compromised for short-term gains.

That trust, once earned, is the most valuable asset and must be protected at all costs. That true strength lies in staying grounded, regardless of how far one progresses.

Today, though he is no longer with us, his vision lives on through every project we undertake and every relationship we build. The Company continues under the leadership of his son, Mr. Sugandh Raj Burman, who carries forward not just a business, but a legacy of integrity and trust.

His life reminds us that foundations built on honesty never fade. They endure.
