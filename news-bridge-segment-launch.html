Bridge segment launch completed ahead of schedule

2026-01-14

Night-shift planning and staged logistics improved productivity and safety compliance.

Sanjeevni Infra completed a bridge segment launch ahead of schedule through detailed night-shift planning and staged logistics. The milestone reflects the value of aligning equipment, labour, access control, and safety supervision before execution begins.

The team used a step-by-step launch plan to reduce movement conflict and maintain quality checks during critical lifting and placement activities. Safety briefings, lighting arrangements, and traffic coordination were built into the work sequence.

Completing the launch ahead of schedule created additional planning flexibility for subsequent activities while maintaining the required safety and quality standards.

Key Focus Areas

  • Ahead-of-schedule bridge segment launch
  • Night-shift planning with safety supervision
  • Staged logistics for equipment and access control
